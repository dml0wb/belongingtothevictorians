Belonging to the Victorians?

Community, Collections, and Care

Friday 4 September 2026

Wilton’s Music Hall, 1 Graces Alley, London E1 8JB

Organised by Daniel Grey & William Bainbridge, University of Hertfordshire

One-Day Conference

This one-day conference brings together researchers working on Victorian community, collections and care. The day comprises four themed panels of short papers, followed by a keynote address from Professor Jane Hamlett (Royal Holloway, University of London).

There is no conference fee, and places are strictly limited.
