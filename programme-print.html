Belonging to the Victorians?

Community, Collections, and Care
Wilton’s Music Hall, 1 Graces Alley, London E1 8JB

A one-day conference bringing together researchers working on Victorian community, collections and care: four themed panels of short papers, followed by a keynote address from Professor Jane Hamlett (Royal Holloway, University of London).

Speakers, please keep your paper to 15 minutes and bring your slides on a USB drive; we will load them panel by panel.
